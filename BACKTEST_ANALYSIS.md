## Entry/Exit Rules

### Entry Signals
1. **Whale Alert**: Flow >$5M in single direction
2. **Gamma Approach**: Price within 0.5% of high gamma strike
3. **Earnings Play**: >$3M flow within 24hrs of earnings
4. **Unusual Flag**: Cheddar notes anomaly

### Exit Signals
1. **Target hit**: 20-30% move in direction
2. **Time stop**: 3-5 days max
3. **Gamma break**: Price clears gamma wall + 1%
4. **Event completion**: Earnings/event passes

### Risk Management
- Position size: 1-2% per signal
- Stop loss: -15% or gamma level breach opposite direction
- Max concurrent: 3 signals
- Avoid: FOMC days, major news events
